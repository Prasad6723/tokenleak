# METRICS

The North Star metric for this product is:

## Completed audits that generate qualified leads

This metric matters because the tool only creates business value when users both complete the audit and demonstrate enough intent to potentially purchase through Credex.

Three input metrics driving this are:

1. Audit completion rate  
How many visitors actually finish the audit flow.

2. Share rate  
How often users share public audit result links.

3. Consultation conversion rate  
How many high-savings users request follow-up discussions.

The first analytics instrumentation I would implement:
- Funnel drop-off tracking
- Tool/plan combinations most commonly audited
- Share button usage
- Email capture conversion
- Time spent on results page

A pivot decision would happen if:
- Audit completion rate stays below 20%
- Share rate remains under 5%
- Consultation requests remain near zero after significant traffic

That would likely indicate the audit either lacks trust, lacks perceived value, or does not produce compelling enough savings recommendations.
